Pool Party at Castell d’Empordà: Saxophone & DJ for the Wedding Day Two

Arnald SAXONARA Thursday, 7 August 2025

The Castell d’Empordà, located in the heart of Girona, is one of the most exclusive venues on the Costa Brava to host luxury weddings and private events. With breathtaking views, historic architecture and a curated selection of suppliers, this castle is a dream setting for international couples who choose Spain for their destination wedding.

For this event, I had the honour of performing at the wedding “Day Two” Pool Party. This celebration, increasingly popular among couples, takes place the day after the wedding: a relaxed gathering around the pool with cocktails, sunshine and, of course, unforgettable music.

unique music concept: live saxophone meets DJ set

For this Pool Party at Castell d’Empordà, I designed a special performance:

  • I started as a DJ, setting the right mood to welcome the guests.

  • At the couple’s entrance, I switched to live saxophone, blending improvised melodies with the energy of the DJ set.

  • Throughout the party, I alternated between saxophone and DJ moments, creating a seamless and vibrant flow.

This hybrid concept allowed me to perform as a solo artist, offering both the atmosphere of a live show and the versatility of a DJ session — the perfect match for a luxury wedding.

Benefits of hiring a solo Saxophonist & DJ

Choosing a professional who combines both live saxophone and DJ skills brings several advantages to any wedding or event:

  1. Cost-effective – One artist covers both live music and DJ set, reducing the need to hire multiple performers.

  2. Seamless atmosphere – The mix of live performance and DJ creates a vibrant, coherent flow throughout the event.

  3. Easier management – A single point of contact for music and technical setup, simplifying coordination for wedding planners and venues.

  4. Full flexibility – The ability to adapt music in real time according to the mood of the guests.

  5. Exclusive image – A stylish combination of saxophone and DJ that surprises and entertains every guest.

Castell d’Empordà: an exclusive venue on the Costa Brava

Known for its elegance and history, Castell d’Empordà has become one of the most prestigious wedding venues in Girona and the Costa Brava. Its luxurious spaces, pool area and high-end services make it the perfect choice for couples looking for a truly unique destination wedding in Spain.

Destination weddings & events in Costa Brava, Girona and Barcelona

As SAXONARA, destination wedding saxophonist and DJ, I perform at exclusive venues across the Costa Brava, Girona and Barcelona, but also in international hotspots such as Mallorca, Ibiza, Marbella, Venice, Lake Como and Milan.

My goal is always the same: to create unforgettable musical experiences that transform each wedding or corporate event into a stylish and extraordinary celebration.
