I like to understand House music not as a trend, but as a space for freedom and musical expression. It's a fascinating meeting point where the most modern rhythms merge with the deep roots of Jazz and Blues. In particular, styles like Deep House and Tropical House surprisingly approach the Cool Jazz popularized by Miles Davis: constant rhythms under an improvised melody full of feeling. This fusion of genres allows me to offer a sound with a current rhythmic base, but with the authenticity and soul of live performance.
