🎷 Stayin’ Alive – Bee Gees Saxophone Cover for Wedding Parties

Arnald SAXONARA Friday, 13 June 2025

Let’s face it — few songs get people moving like "Stayin’ Alive" by the Bee Gees. That funky groove, that iconic intro... and when you bring it to life with live saxophone, the reaction is instant: people jump out of their seats and the party officially begins!

In this video, I share a snippet of my version of “Stayin’ Alive” performed live at a wedding party — one of those timeless tracks that always hits the spot, especially when played on sax in the middle of the dancefloor.

🕺 Live Saxophone for Wedding Parties – Energy, Surprise & Pure Fun

As SAXONARA, I perform during weddings across Spain, France, and Europe, offering high-energy saxophone sets alongside the DJ, especially during the party.

My wedding party format usually includes:

  • 🔥 10-minute live sets spread over 2 hours

  • 🎷 Improvising over disco, house, reggaeton & Ibiza classics
  • 🎉 Surprise entrances at the end of dinner or from unexpected spots (yes, sometimes even a balcony!)
  • 🕺 Making people dance, forming a conga line, or leading guests straight to the party zone

Songs like “Stayin’ Alive” are part of my party playlist, along with hits like:

  • "Destination Calabria" – Alex Gaudino

  • "Freed From Desire" – Gala
  • "September" – Earth, Wind & Fire
  • "Mr. Saxobeat" – Alexandra Stan
  • "One Kiss" – Calvin Harris & Dua Lipa

💍 Available for Weddings in Spain, France & Europe

If you want a wedding party that truly comes alive, with surprise entrances, energy and live music that makes people move, I’d love to join you on the dancefloor.

Based in Barcelona, I perform all over Spain, France, Italy, and for destination weddings across Europe.
