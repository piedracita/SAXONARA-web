Bringing the Party to La Baronia: A Live Sax Performance for a Destination Wedding

Arnald SAXONARA Monday, 22 September 2025

Imagine a stunning, 14th-century manor just an hour from Barcelona, nestled in the serene Catalonian countryside. That’s La Baronia, an exclusive wedding venue renowned for its timeless beauty and as a premier destination for couples from around the world. I recently had the incredible opportunity to perform here, bringing the energy to a spectacular wedding reception.

My role was to elevate the party as a live saxophonist, working alongside the DJ to create an unforgettable atmosphere. I brought my dynamic electronic sax, complete with pulsating lights, to synchronize with the beat and add a visual spectacle to the performance.

The goal was simple: to ignite the dance floor and ensure the party never lost its momentum. Here's a quick video I recorded at La Baronia:

The musical journey was a mix of high-octane tracks and timeless classics. We dropped high-energy electronic music from artists like Avicii and David Guetta, but also wove in the best of past and current disco hits and classic dance tunes that everyone loves. The blend of my live sax melodies and the DJ's seamless mixes created a unique and electrifying vibe that kept guests dancing all night.

This is what I live for—bringing that same level of dynamism to every event, from corporate parties to exclusive destination weddings in Spain, Italy, and France. If you’re planning your special day and want to hire a saxophonist to make it truly unforgettable, let's connect and discuss your vision.
