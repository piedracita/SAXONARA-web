🎷 Destination Wedding at Alcázar del Garraf – Sax, Surprise & Pool Party Vibes near Barcelona

Arnald SAXONARA Monday, 16 June 2025

This summer I had the chance to perform at a truly unique destination wedding in one of the newest and most charming venues near Barcelona: El Alcázar del Garraf, just minutes from Sitges.

The venue has a stunning Mediterranean charm — white façades, vintage touches, palm trees, and that timeless Sitges energy. A perfect blend of elegance and Ibiza-style atmosphere, ideal for couples planning a destination wedding in Spain or even a summer getaway (yes, the venue is also available for holidays).

🎥 In this VLOG, you can see the energy and flow of the wedding day from my perspective as a live saxophonist and entertainer.

🎷 My Performance: From Elegant Banquet to Sunset Party

I began by surprising the guests during the wedding banquet, performing live saxophone between courses. The highlight? Leading the entire group to the party zone with my version of “Jubel” by Klingande — a classic that never fails to create magic.

Once everyone gathered by the pool, the party truly came alive. Together with the DJ, I performed live saxophone over a dynamic and uplifting set of electronic, disco and chill-house hits, including:

  • “Destination Calabria” – Alex Gaudino

  • “September” – Earth, Wind & Fire
  • “No Man No Cry” – Jimmy Sax
  • “Infinity” – Jaymes Young (SAXONARA remix)
  • “Freed from Desire” – Gala

The poolside vibe, the sunset light and the energy from the guests created an unforgettable Ibiza-style celebration — right in the heart of Catalonia.

📍 Are you planning a wedding in Barcelona, Sitges, Ibiza or Mallorca?

If you’re looking for live music with saxophone, elegant but fun, that blends perfectly with your wedding aperitif, dinner or DJ party, I’d love to be part of your day.

Based in Barcelona, I perform regularly in Spain, Ibiza, Mallorca and France, and I specialize in destination weddings with international guests.
