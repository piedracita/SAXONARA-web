🎷 Destination Wedding at Mas Torroella – Live Acoustic SAXONARA Band in Costa Brava

Arnald SAXONARA Saturday, 3 May 2025

Nestled in the hills of Costa Brava, just 1 hour from Barcelona, the spectacular Mas Torroella is one of the most exclusive wedding venues in Spain — and an absolute dream for any couple planning a destination wedding.

In this video, I perform live with my acoustic SAXONARA Band during a beautiful international wedding, offering a sophisticated and soulful soundtrack to the celebration. Whether for the cocktail hour, ceremony, or even roaming during dinner, this live band format brings elegance, warmth and energy — always tailored to the couple’s vibe.

🎶 Live Music at Mas Torroella – The SAXONARA Experience

I’ve had the pleasure of performing at Mas Torroella many times in various formats:

  • 🎷 SAXONARA DJSAX (live sax & DJ set for parties)

  • 🎶 Aperitif sessions with Ibiza chill & modern pop
  • 💍 Ceremony music, emotional and timeless
  • 🕺 Party sax sets with DJ and lights
  • 🎺 Roaming acoustic band
  • 🎙️ And this time: the SAXONARA Band acoustic duo

This particular wedding featured an acoustic set that blended modern love songs with jazzy flair, performed live as guests enjoyed the open-air views and atmosphere of this extraordinary Catalan estate.

💍 Getting Married at Mas Torroella? Make it Unforgettable.

Mas Torroella is a dream location for couples planning a destination wedding in Spain, combining Mediterranean charm, historical beauty and modern luxury.

If you're planning to get married here (or anywhere in Barcelona, Girona, or Costa Brava), I offer a complete musical experience for your day:

  • 🎷 Solo saxophone

  • 🧑‍🎤 Live jazz duo or trio
  • 🕺 DJSAX party performance
