📸 DJSAX Live Meets La Boscana: Luxury Wedding Vibe in Lleida

Arnald SAXONARA Wednesday, 29 October 2025

This week, we are thrilled to share a glimpse behind the scenes from a spectacular destination wedding at La Boscana in Lleida. These incredible photos, captured by the brilliant team at Piña Colada Weddings, truly showcase the magic we created together!

La Boscana: The Perfect Oasis for Luxury Weddings

La Boscana is not just a venue; it’s a breathtaking luxury estate in Lleida, offering an absolutely unique setting for weddings. Surrounded by stunning gardens, exclusive spaces, and renowned for its excellent gastronomy (led by Michelin-starred chef Joel Castañé), it is easily one of the top wedding venues in Lleida and the whole of Catalonia.

The blend of natural beauty and five-star service makes it the ideal backdrop for a memorable celebration.

The Synergy: Saxophone, Energy, and Piña Colada Weddings

Working alongside the talented photographers and videographers from Piña Colada Weddings is always a highlight. Their style—vibrant, dynamic, and focused on capturing genuine moments—is the perfect match for the high-energy DJSAX Live Show.

They excel at crafting compelling visual stories, making them an essential choice for any destination wedding photographer in Spain or Barcelona. If you want a team that captures the vibe, not just the scene, they are the ones to call.

Elevate Your Wedding: Hire a Saxophonist in Lleida

As a professional wedding saxophonist, my role at La Boscana was to soundtrack the elegance of the setting and the joy of the party:

  • 🎷 Cocktail Hour: Setting a sophisticated atmosphere with chilled Deep House and Latin grooves.

  • 🎉 Fiesta: Cranking up the dance floor with the DJ & Sax Show, featuring LED lighting saxophone and live improvisation, ensuring the night ends on an electrifying high note.

If you are planning your wedding at one of the exquisite fincas de boda en Lleida (wedding estates in Lleida) or anywhere in Catalunya, booking a saxophonist for weddings like SAXONARA guarantees the high-impact entertainment your party deserves.
