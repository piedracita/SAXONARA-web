The Musical Selection

My work as a professional saxophonist is based on total adaptability. I don't offer a single style, but a complete sound palette designed to evolve with the different moments of your event and ensure that the atmosphere is always perfect, from the first to the last moment.

My repertoire is extensive and carefully segmented to cover every need: from the most emotive ballads for the Wedding Ceremony, to a Lounge and Chill House style ideal for the Cocktail Hour or Aperitif. And if what you are looking for is maximum energy, my star format is Disco, Funk, and Dance alongside a DJ, perfect for the Open Bar and the party. No matter the atmosphere you seek, I have the perfect soundtrack for the exact moment.
