A Touch of Sax for a Corporate Event in Benicàssim

Arnald SAXONARA Tuesday, 23 September 2025

I recently had the pleasure of performing at a corporate dinner for a German company's incentive trip in Benicàssim, Valencia. The venue, El Invernadero by Grupo Capicúa, provided the perfect setting for a professional yet relaxed event. I traveled by car to the location, which is a fantastic venue for events of this kind.

My performance was designed to match the event's atmosphere perfectly. I brought my live saxophone to the dinner, playing a unique blend of jazz and electronic music. The style was a cool mix of cocktail-lounge at the beginning and chill-out during the dinner, creating an atmosphere that was both elegant and modern. The live music added a fresh and dynamic touch that truly elevated the experience for the guests.

My passion is to bring this unique musical experience to events across Europe. Whether you’re planning a corporate dinner in Valencia or Barcelona, a wedding in Paris, a private party in London, or a special event in Madrid or Milan, I am available to travel and provide an unforgettable live music experience for you and your clients.

If you're looking to hire a saxophonist to elevate your next corporate event or private party, feel free to get in touch. Let's create something special together.
