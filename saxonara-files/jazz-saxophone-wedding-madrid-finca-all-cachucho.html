🎷 Live Jazz & Saxophone for Weddings in Madrid – Fly Me To The Moon at Finca All Cachucho

Arnald SAXONARA Thursday, 1 May 2025

Here’s a glimpse into one of my most elegant and timeless formats: live jazz saxophone and vocals for weddings. In this video, recorded at a beautiful celebration at Finca All Cachucho, just outside Madrid, I performed the iconic classic "Fly Me To The Moon", singing in the style of Frank Sinatra, and playing saxophone — bringing a touch of vintage sophistication to the cocktail hour.

✨ This is a side of SAXONARA not everyone knows: my jazz background, shaped by years of study and performance, from Spain to the U.S. and Puerto Rico. And it fits beautifully in wedding settings — especially during ceremonies, welcome drinks, and aperitifs.

🎶 Jazz Wedding Music – Live Saxophone & Vocals

My jazz repertoire includes classics such as:

  • Fly Me To The Moon

  • Cheek to Cheek
  • L.O.V.E.
  • It Had To Be You
  • The Way You Look Tonight

This performance can be booked in multiple formats:

  • 🎙️ Solo Sax & Voice (with backing tracks)

  • 🎹 Jazz Duo (sax & piano or guitar)
  • 🎷🎻 Jazz Trio (sax, bass, and vocals, or other combinations)

Perfect for:

  • 💍 Weddings (ceremony & cocktail hour)

  • 🍸 Elegant corporate events
  • 🌇 Private soirées & hotel lounges

📍 Available in Madrid and Across Spain

Based in Barcelona, I perform regularly in Madrid, Valencia, Seville, Mallorca, and for destination weddings across Europe. If you're dreaming of a classic, romantic and classy ambiance for your event, jazz may be the perfect touch.
