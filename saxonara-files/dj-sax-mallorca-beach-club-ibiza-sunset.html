Ibiza Sunset Vibes: Bringing the Magic of DJ Sax to Mallorca's Beach Clubs

Gemma GeSaX Saturday, 5 July 2025

There's nothing quite like the energy of a Mallorca beach club as the sun dips below the horizon, painting the sky with fiery hues. Recently, I had the incredible experience of performing a DJ Sax set at one of these stunning venues, creating a vibrant atmosphere that perfectly captured the essence of a Summer Ibiza Sunset with live sax.

My performance blended seamlessly, as I mixed and played over a curated selection of house, tropical house, and other sun-drenched styles. It wasn't just about the music; it was about the experience. I moved through the crowd, interacting with guests, and letting the saxophone guide the flow, crafting that perfect, immersive beach club vibe. It's a truly unique way to elevate any event, offering a dynamic and interactive musical journey.

This kind of live DJ Sax performance is also incredibly popular for wedding pool parties – especially on day two of a destination wedding – and private events seeking that relaxed yet sophisticated party atmosphere. Imagine continuing the celebration by the pool, with the sounds of live sax transforming your gathering into an exclusive, unforgettable experience.

Whether you're planning an event in Mallorca, the other Balearic Islands, or dreaming of a destination wedding in stunning locations across Italy, Spain, or the South of France, my DJ Sax service is designed to bring that high-energy, bespoke musical touch. I travel internationally to create magical moments for clients who value premium, live entertainment.

Gemma GeSaX

Known professionally as GeSaX, Gemma is a talented and versatile female saxophonist based in Ibiza, collaborating regularly with SAXONARA in exclusive events across Europe. From luxury beach clubs and pool parties to high-end destination weddings and private events, she brings elegance, energy and her unique style to every performance.

Her music blends Ibiza house vibes, tropical beats and modern electronic sounds, creating unforgettable atmospheres in every show. Whether performing alongside SAXONARA in a dynamic DJ & Sax duo, or as a solo act, GeSaX moves effortlessly between the DJ booth and the dance floor, interacting with guests and creating the perfect party vibe.

Based in Ibiza, GeSaX travels regularly for events in:

Mallorca and the Balearic Islands
Marbella, Costa del Sol, and southern Spain
Santorini, Lake Como, Tuscany, and other exclusive European destinations
Beyond her performances, GeSaX is also part of the creative team behind the SAXONARA Blog, sharing stories, music insights and behind-the-scenes moments from their international projects.
